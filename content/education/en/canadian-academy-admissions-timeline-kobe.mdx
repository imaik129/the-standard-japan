This is a neutral planning checklist for parents (not official admissions advice). Always confirm current requirements directly with the school.

## Timeline summary

- **12 months out**: contact admissions, define target grade and start date, confirm availability patterns
- **9–6 months out**: tour and ask detailed questions, compare alternatives
- **6–3 months out**: prepare documents and health records, plan language support
- **3–0 months out**: finalize commute, bus, and family logistics

## 12 months out: get the basics right

Start with three decisions:

1. Target grade and start month
2. Home location (commute reality)
3. Language goals (English support + Japanese literacy)

## 6–3 months out: documents and health records

Most international schools require variations of:

- school records / transcripts
- ID / residency documents
- medical and vaccination information
- learning support context (if applicable)

For a general checklist you can reuse across schools:

- [International school application checklist (Japan)](/education/international-school-application-checklist-japan)

## 3–0 months out: logistics that make or break the year

- rush-hour commute test (train / bus / car)
- bus routes and pickup timing
- dual-income schedules and backup pickup plan
- Japanese literacy time at home (if you want long-term Japan options)
