This is a neutral checklist to reduce “hidden surprises” — not a ranking.

## Total cost checklist

| Item | Ask for |
|---|---|
| Monthly fee | frequency-based pricing? |
| Enrollment / registration | first-year cost |
| Materials / uniform | annual extras |
| Events | trips, photos, seasonal fees |
| Bus / transport | route and pricing |
| Refund policy | absence and long breaks |

## Hours and extended care

Dual-income families should confirm:

- open/close time
- extended care availability and cutoffs
- holiday / summer closures
- communication method for late pickup

## Pickup, safety, sick policy

- pickup rules (who can pick up)
- parking / drop-off setup
- illness policy (when children must stay home / return)

## English environment reality

Confirm whether:

- English is used in daily routines (not only lesson blocks)
- beginners are supported without stress

1. Teacher tone and classroom calm  
2. Child transitions (arrival, snack, cleanup)  
3. Parent communication clarity
