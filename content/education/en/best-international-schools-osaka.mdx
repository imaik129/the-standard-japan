Osaka is the economic heart of Kansai — and for international families, it offers a **distinct school market** from nearby Kobe. Some families base themselves in Osaka and commute; others live in Ashiya or Nishinomiya and choose between Osaka and Kobe schools.

## Osaka International School Overview

**Scale:** Smaller than Tokyo, comparable or larger than Kobe depending on how you count programs.

**Commute:** Umeda, Suita, Senri areas — map your home before applying.

**Community:** Strong corporate and academic ties; mix of expat and local international families.

See also: [Best international schools in Japan](/education/best-international-schools-japan)

## Schools to Research

Confirm details on official sites.

**Osaka YMCA International School** — Central-name international program in Osaka; often researched by English-medium families.

**Osaka International School of Kwansei Gakuin (OISKG)** — Connected to Kwansei Gakuin network; check curriculum and grade structure.

**Senri International School** — North Osaka / Senri area; part of Kansai international landscape.

Other families consider **Kyoto** or **Kobe** if commute and lifestyle fit better.

## Osaka vs Kobe for Families

| Factor | Osaka | Kobe |

## Kansai Preschool Note

Families living between Osaka, Ashiya, Nishinomiya, and Kobe may compare education across the wider Kansai area. For younger children, **Peter Pan International Preschool** in Rokko Island, Kobe may be worth considering as an English-speaking preschool option before full international school enrollment. Attendance does not guarantee admission to any international school.

## FAQ

**What are the best international schools in Osaka?**
Osaka YMCA International School, OISKG, and Senri International School are among the frequently researched names. Compare curriculum and commute from your home.

**Should we choose Osaka or Kobe?**
Depends on work location, housing, and school fit. Many Ashiya/Nishinomiya families choose Kobe schools — see [Kobe guide](/education/best-international-schools-kobe).

**Are there international preschools in Osaka?**
Options exist; some families use Kobe English preschools if commute works. See [best preschools in Kobe](/education/best-preschools-kobe).

**How does Osaka compare to Tokyo?**
Tokyo has more schools and higher average fees; Osaka offers Kansai lifestyle with fewer options.
