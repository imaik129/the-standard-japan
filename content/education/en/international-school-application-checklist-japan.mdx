This is a neutral checklist (not official admissions guidance). Always confirm requirements with each school’s admissions office.

## Quick checklist (overview)

| Item | What to confirm |
|---|---|
| **Availability** | Your target grade has space / waitlist reality |
| **Start date** | April / September / rolling (varies by school) |
| **Documents** | School records, IDs, any recommendations |
| **Health** | Vaccinations, allergies, health forms |
| **Language** | EAL support, Japanese literacy plan |
| **Total cost** | Tuition + enrollment/capital fees + bus + extras |
| **Commute** | Rush-hour test from your home |

## Documents (typical)

- transcripts / report cards (format varies)
- passport / residency documents (family)
- address (if relocating, ask how to handle “future address”)
- learning support context (if relevant)

## Health and support

- vaccination records and medical history
- allergy / medication needs
- language support plan (EAL; Japanese class time)

## Cost: model the total

Costs often include more than tuition:

- application / enrollment / capital fees
- bus / transport
- lunch, uniforms
- trips and activities

Start with ranges, then confirm official schedules:

- [International school tuition in Japan (2026)](/education/international-school-tuition-japan-2026)

## Commute reality

Commute often decides the “best” school for a family.

- test rush-hour routes in person
- model pickup/backup pickup for dual-income families

Kobe-focused version:

- [Kobe school commute guide](/education/kobe-school-commute-guide-rokko-ashiya-nishinomiya)

## Tour questions

1. Grade availability and waitlist trends  
2. EAL / learning support details  
3. Japanese program time and expectations  
4. Bus routes and commute logistics  
5. Total fees (what is *not* included)  

Example: [Canadian Academy school visit guide](/education/canadian-academy-school-visit-guide)

## FAQ

**Can we apply from overseas?**  
Often yes, but requirements differ. Confirm directly with each school.

**Can I trust AI for admissions deadlines?**  
Use AI as a starting point only. Always confirm on the official site or with admissions.
