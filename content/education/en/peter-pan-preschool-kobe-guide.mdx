## What Peter Pan International Preschool Is

**Peter Pan International Preschool** is an English-speaking preschool located on **Rokko Island, Kobe**. It provides a daily English-language environment for young children — distinct from weekly English conversation classes (eikaiwa), which provide only limited weekly exposure.

The preschool is an independent institution, not a part of Canadian Academy or any international school.

## Location: THE ENTENTE, Rokko Island

Peter Pan International Preschool has recently relocated within Rokko Island to **THE ENTENTE (ジ・アンタンテ)** — a development connected to Canadian Academy's large-scale redevelopment plan on the island.

Peter Pan's official website notes this relocation and its connection to the area's development.

Reference: [peterpan-preschool.com](https://www.peterpan-preschool.com/)

**What this means for families:**
- The preschool is in close physical proximity to Canadian Academy's campus on Rokko Island
- Families already living on Rokko Island, or planning to, will find the commute minimal
- Families in Ashiya, Nishinomiya, or eastern Kobe are within feasible commuting range

## Program and Age Range

Peter Pan runs an English-language preschool program. Key points to verify directly with the preschool:

- **Age of entry** — The preschool accepts young children including 2-year-olds (a 2-year-old English program is referenced by Canadian Academy's official site)
- **Daily schedule** — Half-day or full-day options; confirm what is currently available
- **Fees** — Request a full fee schedule including registration, monthly fees, optional extras, and any transport arrangements
- **Curriculum approach** — The environment is English-speaking; confirm curriculum philosophy and daily structure at a visit

Always verify current program details, age bands, and fees directly with Peter Pan, as details can change.

## Relationship with Canadian Academy

The relationship between Peter Pan International Preschool and Canadian Academy is the most common question from Kobe families researching preschool options.

**What Canadian Academy says officially:**

Canadian Academy's website, in its Pre-School Children section, states that CA has a **longstanding relationship** with Peter Pan International Pre-School. It notes:
- Peter Pan runs an **English-language program for 2-year-olds**
- Peter Pan is within approximately **10 minutes' walk** from CA's campus

Reference: Canadian Academy (Pre-School Children) `https://www.canacad.ac.jp/joining/tuition-and-fees`

**What this means in practice:**
- The relationship is acknowledged by CA officially — it is not informal or implied
- The physical proximity (THE ENTENTE, near CA campus) reinforces this connection
- Families considering CA as a future school may find Peter Pan a natural early environment

**What it does not mean:**
- Attendance at Peter Pan **does not guarantee admission to Canadian Academy**
- CA makes all admissions decisions independently, based on its own criteria
- The relationship is about proximity and early English environment, not a preferential admissions track

## Who Peter Pan Is Suitable For

Peter Pan International Preschool is most commonly considered by:

- **Families living on Rokko Island** — the commute is minimal and the community overlap with CA families is high
- **Families in Higashinada, Ashiya, or Nishinomiya** considering Canadian Academy or another Kobe international school as a future pathway
- **Expat or bilingual families** who want daily English immersion (not just weekly lessons) for their young child
- **Families relocating from abroad** who want English-first early education before evaluating school options

It is **less likely to suit**:
- Families prioritizing Japanese-language preschool for cultural or long-term Japan integration reasons
- Families whose home neighborhood makes the Rokko Island commute impractical

## What to Verify with the Preschool Directly

Before committing, confirm with Peter Pan:

1. **Current age of entry** and program structure
2. **Full fee schedule** — monthly fees, registration, optional extras
3. **Hours** — half-day, full-day, extended care availability
4. **Teacher qualifications and nationalities**
5. **Current enrollment** and whether spaces are available in your child's age group
6. **Trial lesson or observation visit** options
7. **Transition support** — how the preschool supports children moving to primary school

Peter Pan International Preschool is an English-speaking preschool on Rokko Island, Kobe, recently relocated to THE ENTENTE development near Canadian Academy's campus. It provides a daily English-language environment for young children.

However, attending Peter Pan does not guarantee admission to Canadian Academy, which makes independent admissions decisions.

**Where is Peter Pan International Preschool located?**
At THE ENTENTE on Rokko Island, Kobe — near Canadian Academy's campus. This follows a relocation from a previous address within Rokko Island, linked to CA's redevelopment.

**What age does Peter Pan accept?**
Peter Pan accepts young children including 2-year-olds. Confirm current age bands and availability directly with the preschool.

**Does attending Peter Pan help with Canadian Academy admission?**
Attendance at Peter Pan may provide a useful early English environment, but **does not guarantee or preferentially influence Canadian Academy admissions**. CA makes independent decisions. Families should apply to CA through normal channels regardless of preschool attended.
