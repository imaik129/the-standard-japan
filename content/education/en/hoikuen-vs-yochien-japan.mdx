This is a neutral overview. Rules and availability vary by city/ward — always verify with your municipality.

## Quick comparison

| Topic | Hoikuen (daycare) | Yochien (kindergarten) |
|---|---|---|
| Primary fit | working-parent childcare | education rhythm + shorter hours |
| Hours | often longer | often shorter |
| Pickup logistics | core decision factor | still important |
| Availability | varies strongly by area | varies strongly by area |

## How families decide (practical order)

1. **Work schedule**: can you handle shorter hours and more pickup?  
2. **Commute reality**: home → childcare → work flow  
3. **Child readiness**: transitions, naps, routine  
4. **Local availability**: what exists near your home

## FAQ

**Is hoikuen always better for working parents?**  
Often, but not always — some families use extended care options elsewhere. Confirm locally.
