Hook sentence. Bold claim or contrarian take. Make the reader want to keep going.

Second sentence that reinforces the thesis. Set expectations for what they'll learn.

## First Section (H2)

Opening paragraph with specific details. Use **bold** for key terms and recommendations. Include names, places, prices when relevant.

Second paragraph with more depth. Each section should be 2-4 paragraphs.

**Specific recommendation** — Detail with context. Format like this for scannability.

**Another point** — More specifics. The more concrete, the better.

## Second Section

Continue the structure. Every H2 should be something a reader might search for or skim to.

- Bullet points for lists
- Keep items specific and actionable
- Names, addresses, prices

## Third Section

More content. Aim for 1,500+ words for comprehensive guides.

## Fourth Section

Keep going. 4-6 H2 sections is ideal for SEO and readability.

## FAQ

**What's the best X?**
Direct answer in 1-3 sentences. Be specific. This format gets extracted for AI/featured snippets.

**How much does X cost?**
Include price ranges. ¥800–¥1,200 or similar.

**Do I need to speak Japanese?**
Practical answer. Yes/no with nuance.

**When is the best time to visit?**
Specific times, seasons, or conditions.

**Is X worth it?**
Opinion with reasoning. Your voice matters.

**Can I do X without Y?**
Address common concerns. Be helpful.
